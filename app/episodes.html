Episode Guide

Explore memorable episodes from My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic

Friendship is Magic, Part 1 & 2

Season 1, Episodes 1-2

Twilight Sparkle arrives in Ponyville to oversee the Summer Sun Celebration and meets five new friends. Together, they must stop Nightmare Moon and restore Princess Luna.

The Ticket Master

Season 1, Episode 3

When Princess Celestia gives Twilight two tickets to the Grand Galloping Gala, all her friends want to go, creating a dilemma about who should get the extra ticket.

Boast Busters

Season 1, Episode 6

The Great and Powerful Trixie comes to Ponyville showing off her magic skills, challenging anypony to prove they're better than her.

Winter Wrap Up

Season 1, Episode 11

Twilight tries to help with the annual Winter Wrap Up in Ponyville, but without magic, she struggles to find her place in the earth pony tradition.

The Return of Harmony, Part 1 & 2

Season 2, Episodes 1-2

Discord, the spirit of chaos, breaks free from his stone prison and turns Equestria upside down. The mane six must use the Elements of Harmony to stop him.

A Canterlot Wedding, Part 1 & 2

Season 2, Episodes 25-26

Princess Cadance is getting married, but Twilight suspects something is wrong with the bride. Her suspicions lead to the discovery of Queen Chrysalis and the changelings.

Magical Mystery Cure

Season 3, Episode 13

When Twilight accidentally switches her friends' cutie marks and destinies, she must find a way to fix her mistake and discovers her own destiny as an alicorn princess.

Twilight's Kingdom, Part 1 & 2

Season 4, Episodes 25-26

Lord Tirek escapes from Tartarus and begins stealing magic from all ponies. Princess Twilight must learn to use her role as a princess to save Equestria.

The Cutie Map, Part 1 & 2

Season 5, Episodes 1-2

The mane six discover a village where everypony has given up their cutie marks to Starlight Glimmer, who preaches about the evils of being different.

The Last Problem

Season 9, Episode 26

In the series finale, we see the future of our beloved ponies as Twilight prepares to take over as ruler of Equestria from Princess Celestia and Princess Luna.
